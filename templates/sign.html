Athlete Recruiting Services Agreement

1. Services Provided

Collegiate Athletic Planning (CAP) agrees to provide recruiting services to the Athlete named above, including but not limited to: profile development, video distribution, direct outreach to college coaching staffs, scheduling of campus visits, and ongoing recruiting guidance throughout the process.

2. Athlete Responsibilities

The Athlete agrees to: (a) maintain academic eligibility per NCAA/NAIA requirements; (b) provide accurate and current information including GPA, test scores, and athletic statistics; (c) communicate promptly with CAP staff regarding interest levels, visit scheduling, and offer decisions; and (d) notify CAP immediately upon making a verbal or written commitment to any institution.

3. Term

This Agreement is effective upon the date signed below and continues until the Athlete signs a National Letter of Intent (NLI) or otherwise concludes the recruiting process. Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice.

4. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality regarding any non-public information shared during the course of services, including but not limited to communication logs, offer details, and strategic plans developed by CAP on behalf of the Athlete.

5. Limitation of Liability

CAP does not guarantee placement at any particular institution. Our services are advisory in nature. CAP shall not be liable for decisions made by college coaching staffs or admissions offices. Total liability under this agreement shall not exceed the fees paid to CAP by the Athlete.

6. Agreement

By signing below, the Athlete (and parent/guardian if under 18) acknowledges they have read, understood, and agree to all terms of this Recruiting Services Agreement.
