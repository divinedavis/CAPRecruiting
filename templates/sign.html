Services Provided

CAP agrees to provide the following services to the Client: collegiate athletic planning and consulting services, including but not limited to promoting the athlete to college coaches through CAP connections, guidance on collegiate athletic eligibility requirements, assistance with college recruitment strategies, advising on athletic scholarship opportunities, and additional services as agreed upon by both parties in writing.

Term and Payment Terms

The term of this Agreement shall be for one-year from the effective start date. If this term begins and runs into your senior season, this contract will continue through the end of February of your senior year of high school.

If the term is over one-year in length, the client agrees to pay the extra length of the term at the monthly rate through February of the athlete's senior season. If the athlete is entering their senior year and the term will be less than a year, the client agrees to pay any remaining amount through continuous monthly payments or an agreed payment plan.

Payment Options

1. Monthly Payment: $600 total, payable in monthly installments of $50.
2. One Time Payment: $600 for the 12 months

Payment Schedule

First monthly payment is due at time of sign up. Monthly invoices will be sent on the date of signing up in the proceeding months until contract completion. Invoices are to be paid within 7 days of the invoice sent date. Full payment is due at time of sign up.

Termination

The Client agrees to a one-year commitment with CAP. If the Client chooses to terminate this Agreement prior to the completion of the one-year term, the Client shall pay 50% of the remaining balance of the contract.

Refunds

Once payments are made, no refunds will be given under any circumstances.
